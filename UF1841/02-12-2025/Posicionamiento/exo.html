A community wiki and technical datasheet for micro:bit. Open-source content maintained by the Micro:bit Educational Foundation to document hardware and software. Contributions help broaden the ecosystem and improve resources for developers and educators.

The micro:bit developer community site

This site aims to serve as both a community wiki and a technical datasheet for the BBC micro:bit. It is open-source, available on GitHub under a Creative Commons license, and maintained by the Micro:bit Educational Foundation to document hardware and software related to micro:bit. We welcome contributions to expand and strengthen the ecosystem.
